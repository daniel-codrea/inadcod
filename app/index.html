Hi, I'm Daniel and I am a web and graphic designer based in Cluj Napoca, Romania. I specialise in front-end development and web design.

 

I write perfectly semantic and seo friendly HTML5 code enhanced with CSS3 and jQuery, using best practice accessibility and the latest W3C web standards guidelines. All my websites are hand coded.

For building websites my weapons of choice are Photoshop, Illustrator and Fireworks for creating layouts, then SublimeText and/or Brackets to produce clean semantic HTML and CSS. For projects that require content management, my CMS of choice are Wordpress and Joomla.

I'm always learning new stuff and building on my prior knowledge, that's why most of my time is spent in front of my computer :)

I am currently available for freelance work. You can contact me at info@inadcod.com or use this form.
